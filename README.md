## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Pizza Sales Dashboard** built using **SQL** and **Power BI**. The objective was to analyze pizza sales data, uncover meaningful business insights, and help decision-makers understand customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.

The dashboard combines KPI reporting, trend analysis, and interactive visualizations to transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary goals of this project were to:

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify best-selling and worst-selling pizzas.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Discover daily and monthly sales trends.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📈 Business Insights

### 📅 Sales Trends

- Friday generated the highest number of customer orders.
- Weekend evenings experienced peak demand.
- July recorded the highest monthly sales.

### 🍕

Classic Pizza category generated the highest overall sales.

### 📏

Large-sized pizzas contributed the highest revenue.

### ⭐ Best Sellers

- Thai Chicken Pizza
- Barbecue Chicken Pizza
- California Chicken Pizza
- Classic Deluxe Pizza
- Pepperoni Pizza

### 📉 Worst Sellers

- Brie Carre Pizza
- Mediterranean Pizza
- Spinach Supreme Pizza
- Spinach Pesto Pizza

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for top-selling pizzas.
- Schedule additional staff during weekends.
- Promote low-performing pizzas through discounts and combo offers.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing pizza categories.
- Maintain sufficient stock of Large-sized pizzas.
